Ingredients

  • 1kg potatoes
  • 2l oil
  • mustard(optional)
  • seasoning
  • ketchup

preparation steps

  1. peel the potatoes and cut them into batonnet or julienne-cut.wash and rinse then leave to dry
  2. put a pot on a lit stove and add oil to it
  3. boil the oil and add the cut potatoes
  4. let em deep fry till crispy
  5. serve them in a plate and sprinkle the seasoning add ketchup
